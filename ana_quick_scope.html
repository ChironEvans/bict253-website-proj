Because there is travel on your primary weapon when unscoped, it can be unsuitable for use at long ranges. However, the movement speed penalty incurred when scoped in can make you an easy target. The solution to this is quick scoping, which will allow you to utilise the instant travel time of scoped fire without the movement penalty. Hold scope to begin zooming, and as soon as it is scoped it, fire and unscope.

Time to Cross the Gap:

Unscoped: 1.75s
Scoped: 3.25s
Quick Scoping: 1.66s
